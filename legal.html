Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Liga PH respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our digital street basketball ecosystem.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Data: Name, email address, skill level, height, and preferred position.
  • Location Data: Your city and precise location data when using the "Find Local Games" map feature.
  • Performance Data: Game attendance, user ratings, commendations, and squad match scores.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to facilitate basketball games, calculate community ELO rankings, and maintain the integrity of the player rating system. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

3. Visibility

By default, your display name, skill level, and reliability ratings are public to other authenticated users on the platform to ensure transparency in matchmaking.

Terms of Play

By registering for Liga PH, you agree to abide by the following Terms of Play, designed to keep the community safe, competitive, and respectful.

1. Assumption of Risk

Basketball is a physical contact sport. By participating in games organized via Liga PH, you acknowledge and assume all risks associated with physical injury. Liga PH is a software platform and is not liable for injuries sustained on the court.

2. Code of Conduct

We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy for physical violence, racism, discrimination, or targeted harassment. Users reported for fighting will face a permanent hardware and IP ban from the platform.

3. Account Integrity

Users may only maintain one active account. "Smurfing" (creating alternate accounts to manipulate skill ratings) is prohibited and will result in the deletion of all associated accounts.

Rules & Regulations

Unless specifically noted by the game organizer, all pickup games hosted on Liga PH default to the standard Filipino streetball rule set.

Standard Pickup Rules

  • 1. Call Your Own Fouls: The offensive player calls their own fouls. No blood, no foul rule does not apply; respect the call.
  • 2. Scoring: Games are typically played to 15 or 21, scoring 1s and 2s. Win by 2 is standard unless agreed otherwise.
  • 3. Clear It: On every change of possession (rebounds, steals), the ball must be cleared past the three-point line.
  • 4. Check Ball: The ball must be checked at the top of the key after every dead ball or foul call.

Tournament & Squad Matches

Official 5v5 Squad Matches operate under standard FIBA rules with assigned referees. Final scores must be logged via the app by the winning captain within 2 hours of game completion.

How Ratings Work

The Liga PH rating system is designed to provide an accurate reflection of a player's skill, reliability, and character on the court.

trending_up

Skill Ratings (1-5 Stars)

After a game, players can rate their teammates on Shooting, Passing, Dribbling, Rebounding, and Defense. Your global average determines your tier (Novice to Elite).

psychology

Character Ratings

Basketball is a team game. Players are rated on Sportsmanship, Attitude, and Punctuality. Low character scores will trigger matchmaking penalties.

The Reliability Score

Your Reliability Score is the most important metric on Liga PH. It is calculated automatically based on your attendance.

(Games Attended / Games Joined) * 100 = Reliability %.

Players who consistently "No-Show" will be restricted from joining highly competitive games.
